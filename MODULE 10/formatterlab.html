🛠️ Text Styling Workshop

Welcome to our workshop where we explore different ways to format and enhance text using HTML. Each section demonstrates a unique text formatting element.

Bold and Strong Text

The elephant is the largest land mammal in the world.

You should follow safety instructions carefully when working in a laboratory.

Italic and Emphasized Text

The botanical name of the sunflower is Helianthus annuus.

It is extremely important to save your work regularly while using a computer.

Highlighted and Small Text

Key Fact: the Earth revolves around the Sun once every year.

The Moon is about 384,400 km away from Earth. (average distance)

Deleted Text Example

Bats are birds mammals.

The Amazon River is the longest river in the world. It is actually one of the longest rivers, depending on measurement methods.

Superscript and Subscript Examples

The formula for the area of a square is s2.

Carbon dioxide is represented as CO2.

The cube of a number can be written as n3.
