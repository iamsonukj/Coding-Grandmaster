🐬 Fact 1: The Remarkable Dolphin

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their communication skills, playful behavior, and strong social bonds.

Why are dolphins so intelligent?

Dolphins can learn complex tasks, recognize themselves in mirrors, and communicate using a variety of sounds and signals.

🌋 Fact 2: Powerful Volcanoes

There are more than 1,500 potentially active volcanoes around the world, and many continue to shape Earth's surface.

How hot is lava?

Lava temperatures can range from 700°C to 1,200°C, making it hot enough to melt many types of rock.

Do volcanoes always erupt explosively?

No. Some volcanoes produce gentle lava flows, while others create powerful eruptions that release ash and gases into the atmosphere.

🌲 Fact 3: The Wonders of Forests

Forests cover nearly one-third of Earth's land area and provide habitats for countless plants and animals.

Why are forests important?

Forests help produce oxygen, absorb carbon dioxide, regulate climate, and support biodiversity across the planet.

Interesting Comparison

A single mature tree can absorb approximately 22 kilograms of carbon dioxide each year, helping to reduce greenhouse gases.
